A small business owner that I previously worked for wanted a website to help advertise and provide a way for potential clients to contact him. His business was still in its infant stages, so a low cost solution was required. After comparing cost structures of different services, I decided to use Amazon Web Services. To keep cost down I developed using a serverless framework and integrated a contact form by using Amazon Lambda with their simple email service.
